
We’ve gone to town on this at the restaurant, using Taittinger Comtes de 
Champagne 2006 
<http://www.wine-searcher.com/find/taittinger+comte+de+blanc+brut+champagne+france/2006/uk>
 andHennessy Paradis cognac <http://www.hennessy.com/us/collection/paradis/>, 
neither of which is cheap. Just apply good taste when picking substitutes. 
Serves one.

1 cane sugar cube
4 drops Angostura bitters
15ml cognac
5ml Grand Marnier
100ml champagne
Orange peel and gold leaf (optional), to garnish

Put the sugar in a chilled flute glass, add the bitters and soak. Pour on the 
cognac and Grand Marnier, top with champagne, garnish, and sip away.
