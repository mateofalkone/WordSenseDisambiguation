Fees The majority of full-time undergraduate tuition fees are £8,750 or £9,000 
per year for the year 2016-17. Fees for foundation degrees delivered off-campus 
at partner colleges are £7,500.

Tuition fees for part-time students are £4,500 per 120 credits.

The tuition fees for full-time international students vary by course.

Bursaries A range of scholarships are on offer, including awards for students 
who have attended partner colleges. All full-time first year undergraduate Home 
and EU students can enjoy free travel on Metro, bus, ferry and some Northern 
Rail services in their Fresher year, or £600 discount onUniversity of Sunderland
 <http://www.theguardian.com/education/universityofsunderland> accommodation.

In addition, full-time students in second, third and fourth year can enjoy 
discounted travel through the get around travel scheme. Figures quoted are for 
2016-17, for updates and more information visitsunderland.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.sunderland.ac.uk/ug/feesandfunding/sunderlandscholarships201617/>.
