
Samatoa.com <http://www.samatoa.com/PrestaShop/>

Samatoa is a Cambodian company specialising in the weaving and printing of 
silk. Its aim is to teach Cambodian women new skills in pattern-cutting, 
printing, weaving and sewing and thus improve their lives.

You can either buy garments straight off the site or send them your 
measurements and buy a custom-made piece.
