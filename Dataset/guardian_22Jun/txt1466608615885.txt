
Supported by the Anglo American Group Foundation 
<http://www.angloamericangroupfoundation.org/>, Pro Mujer <http://promujer.org/>
 is an international, women’s development and microfinance NGO. Established in 
1990 and focused on alleviating poverty across Latin America, Pro Mujer 
trailblazes new paths for low-income women in Latin America. The organisation 
aims to fuel leadership, empower women to transform their lives, and to ignite 
greater social and economic opportunities for families. This is achieved by 
providing access to financial services, education and training, and lifesaving 
health care. To learn more about Pro Mujer’s work and to join the community,
click here <http://promujer.org/>.
