Fee £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17. Fees and funding 
options for international (non-EU) and part-time students will vary, visit
port.ac.uk/tuitionfees 
<http://www.port.ac.uk/departments/services/academicregistry/studentadministrationdivision/studentadministrationteam/tuitionfees>
 for more information.

Bursaries Means-tested bursaries are offered for students whose household is 
up to £32,000.

Visit port.ac.uk/nationalbursaryscheme 
<http://www.port.ac.uk/departments/services/academicregistry/studentadministrationdivision/studentadministrationteam/nationalbursaryscheme/>
 for more information.

Accommodation Estimated prices range from £90 per week (self-catered shared 
bathroom) to £161 per week (catered en-suite), £3,600 to £6,440 per year. New 
UK and EU full-time applicants who firmly accept an offer to study at the
University of Portsmouth 
<http://www.theguardian.com/education/universityofportsmouth> will be offered a 
room in halls if they meet required accommodation deadlines. International 
students are also guaranteed a place if they meet specific deadlines.
