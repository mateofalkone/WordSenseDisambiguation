Fees £9,000 for home and EU full-time students; £10,700 for international 
students studying the arts or social sciences; £12,500 for international 
students studying the sciences. Figures quoted are for the academic year 
2016-17, checkaber.ac.uk/fees-finance 
<https://www.aber.ac.uk/en/undergrad/fees-finance/tuition-fees/> for updates.

 Welsh and EU students are entitled to a tuition fee grant of up to £5,100 
which means that eligible Welsh and EU students will actually borrow £3,900 
(not £9,000) per year in 2016-17.

Bursaries and scholarships Around 80% of students receive an award of some 
kind, such as anentrance scholarships or merit award 
<https://www.aber.ac.uk/en/undergrad/fees-finance/scholarships/uk-eu/entrance-merit/>
, which have a value of up to £3,600 over 3 years. Visit 
www.aber.ac.uk/en/scholarships/uk <http://www.aber.ac.uk/en/scholarships/uk> 
for full details


Accommodation Guaranteed for first years. Weekly rates range from £85.40 
(self-catered, single room within a self-contained flat accommodating 6-10 
students) to £124.88 (spacious studio apartment for single occupancy, including 
a private study bedroom, shower room and kitchen with dining area).
