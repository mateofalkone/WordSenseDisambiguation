Fees If you are a full-time student from Scotland and you satisfy the 
eligibility requirements, you will have your tuition fees paid by the Scottish 
Government through theStudents <http://www.theguardian.com/education/students> 
Awards Agency for Scotland (SAAS).

For students from the rest of the UK, fees are charged at £9,000 for full-time 
undergraduates in 2016-17. If you are from elsewhere in the EU you are entitled 
to be charged the same level of tuition fees as Scottish students.


International student fees vary.

Bursaries/Scholarships Means-tested awards and academic excellence 
scholarships are available. Visithw.ac.uk/scholarships 
<http://www.hw.ac.uk/scholarships> for more information.

Accommodation All full-time undergraduate students including local residents 
who apply by 25 August are guaranteed university-owned accommodation. Prices 
range from £105.84 for a self-catered single bedroom (9sqm) with wash hand 
basin, to £159.74 for a self-catered en-suite (15sqm) with a private bathroom 
and large kitchen shared with five other students.
