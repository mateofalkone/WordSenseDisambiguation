But we here at GuardianWitness say, why stop there? With the referendum fast 
approaching, what do tortoises, goldfish and pet rabbits think? What is the 
mood in Britain’s guinea pig community? Perhaps there are other dogs and cats 
whose voices are being ignored by the mainstream media?
