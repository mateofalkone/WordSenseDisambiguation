Fees £9,000 for home/EU full-time students. For part-time students, £5,925 per 
year for a four-year course or £3,950 per year for a six-year course and £988 
per 15 credits in 2016-17.

For international students, most courses range from £11,750 to £12,250, 
although some business and law courses cost more. Find out more at
dmu.ac.uk/intfeesandfunding <http://dmu.ac.uk/intfeesandfunding>

Bursaries DMU offers a package of financial support. Visit 
dmu.ac.uk/fees-and-funding/dmu-scholarships-and-bursaries 
<http://www.dmu.ac.uk/dmu-students/the-student-gateway/student-finance-and-welfare/fees-and-funding/dmu-scholarships-and-bursaries.aspx>
 for more information.

Accommodation DMU aims to provide university-sourced accommodation for all new 
full-time undergraduate and international students who want it. In 2016-17 
prices ranged from £92 to £162 a week.
