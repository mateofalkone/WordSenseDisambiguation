Fees £9,000 for full-time home/EU undergraduates in 2016-17.

Fees for international students vary, visit the university’s course search tool
 <http://www.lancaster.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/courses/course-search/> for 
more information.

Bursaries/Scholarships A variety of awards available, including: an academic 
scholarship of £2,000 for the first year of study to any student from the UK 
entering with A*A*A or equivalent academic qualifications.

An access scholarship of £1,000 a year for all UK students from households 
with an income of less than £42,600 who achieve grades of A*AA or the 
equivalent academic qualifications is also available.

A Lancaster bursary of £1,000 per annum is offered to all students from the UK 
with a household income of less than £42,600.

For more information on bursaries and scholarships at Lancaster, visit 
lancaster.ac.uk/scholarships-and-bursaries 
<http://www.lancaster.ac.uk/study/undergraduate/fees-and-funding/scholarships-and-bursaries/>
.

Accommodation Accommodation is guaranteed for all students who put Lancaster 
as their first choice, firmly accept an offer from the university, and apply 
before the deadline.

Rent for single study bedrooms in 2016–17 ranges from £85.40 to £151.55 per 
week.
