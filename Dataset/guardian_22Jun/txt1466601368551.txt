
It’s award season and in the lead-up to the Baftas there’s a buzz in the air 
about all things dramatic.

Glasgow is a hub for famous acting talent with leading lights such as Alan 
Cumming (Golden Globe nominee, best actor) and Sam Heughan (Golden Globe 
nominee, best TV series), who trained in Glasgow at the Royal Conservatoire of 
Scotland (RCS).

RCS acting tutor Ali de Souza knows what to look for in a successful audition. 
In this video he gives a masterclass in how to prepare for an audition and 
gives advice on techniques for budding actors to get the most from their 
creative potential.
