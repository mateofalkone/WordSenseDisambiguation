Fees £9,000 for full-time undergraduates in 2016-17. Fees for home/EU 
part-time students are £1,125 per 15 credit module or £2,250 per 30 credit 
module in 2016-2017

International student fees are £10,700 full time in 2016-2017

Bursaries Subject-specific bursaries, support for care leavers and mature 
learner funds are available. Visitwrittle.ac.uk/HE-Scholarships-&-Bursaries 
<http://www.writtle.ac.uk/HE-Scholarships-&-Bursaries-16-17> for more 
information.

Accommodation There is a range of room types from large singe en-suite to 
standard rooms. Fees start at £3,984 per year.
